About Us

Our Commitment to Quality and Heritage

Experience Our Unique Approach

With a rich history dating back to the invention of the muselet in 1844, our company continues a tradition of innovation.

We leverage cutting-edge technology, like the pioneering spirit of the first semi-automatic production machines developed in Italy, to deliver world-class wirehoods. Our focus is on providing secure, reliable, and elegant solutions for the world's finest beverage producers.
